Introduction of Secnettool for macOS

What is VPN? A VPN, or virtual private network, is a secure tunnel between two or more devices. VPNs are used to protect private web traffic from snooping, interference, and censorship. What is Secnettool? Secnettool is an IPsec VPN App, which encrypts your network traffic, so that nobody between you and the VPN server can eavesdrop on your data as it travels via the Internet.
